Caring for you during COVID-19

We are delighted to offer a full service to our patients. We’re still open to care for your smiles, with our services unaffected by the latest government COVID-19 restrictions.

Our practice is part of the Bupa Dental Care Group and we would like to reassure you that we’re doing everything we can to keep our patients safe. You can find out more about the extra safety measures we’ve introduced and what to expect next time you visit us from our COVID-19 safety charter and short video.

We are continuing to experience increased patient demand, so please bear with us if we are unable to book you in as quickly as normal. You can email us on info@77harleystreet.co.uk and a member of our team will contact you as soon as possible.

Thank you for your patience and understanding.

Opening Times

We are open from 9am to 5pm, Monday to Friday.

Please call 020 7580 9541 to make an appointment - if you are calling after hours, please leave a message and we will contact you the following working day.

In Case of an Out of Hours Emergency, please call our office on 020 7580 9541 we will get back to you as soon as we can for advice or to refer you to our emergency dentist.
